Thrills and Chills for Everyone!

King Twister is the tallest, fastest, and longest ride in the entire park! Experience the heart-pounding thrills
of the tower of doom as your dropped from 200 ft in the air; feel the speed with which you sent down on our newest
water ride Canyon Ridge Rapids. Little too exciting? Try the Serpent's Way with its less extreme but thrilling launch
that send riders soaring slipping rounds bends in the spirit of its namesake. Want to enjoy some fun with the whole
family? You're welcome to hop on to The Twirling Crown, Jelly Fish Go-Around and the Prancing Pony Parade.
